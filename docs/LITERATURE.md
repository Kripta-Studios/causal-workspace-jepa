## Operationalized workspace tests

- Jacobian transport: compare prompt-local, corpus-averaged, learned nonlinear, and sparse-feature
  transports under held-out interventions.
- Reportability and voluntary control: test only where an explicit report/control channel exists;
  neither is assumed for a JEPA latent.
- Causal mediation and selective necessity: execute candidate removal/patching and compare with
  dimension-, norm-, density-, and manifold-matched controls.
- Flexible reuse and broadcast: require a compact subspace to be read by prediction, uncertainty,
  value, risk, planning, and action consumers on held-out tasks.
- Circuit reconstruction: require necessity, sufficiency, specificity, faithfulness, minimality,
  and replication. Both current aggregate circuit candidates are rejected.

No entry in this registry licenses a claim of consciousness, sentience, or literal equivalence
between Anthropic's J-space and a JEPA workspace candidate.
